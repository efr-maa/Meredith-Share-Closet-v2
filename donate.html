Give Items a Second Life

Meredith Share Closet accepts clean and gently used clothing, footwear, dorm supplies, and books. Your donation can help another student save money and feel supported on campus.

A Message About Donating

Watch this short message to learn why donating useful items can make a difference in the Meredith community.

Donation Guidelines

  • Items should be clean and gently used.
  • Clothing should be washed before donating.
  • Books and school supplies should be in usable condition.
  • Do not donate broken, unsafe, or heavily damaged items.

Before You Donate

  • Wash clothing before donating.
  • Make sure items are safe and usable.
  • Include size information when possible.
  • Do not donate broken or heavily damaged items.

Helpful Tip

If you are unsure about an item, describe it clearly in the form so the closet can decide if it can be accepted.
