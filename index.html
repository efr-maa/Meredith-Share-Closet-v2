Our Mission

The goal of Meredith Share Closet is to reduce waste, support students, and encourage kindness on campus. Instead of throwing away useful items, students can give them a second life.

Featured Categories

Clothing

Find jackets, shirts, dresses, and everyday wear.

Dorm Items

Get useful items for your room and campus living.

How It Works

1. Browse

Look through available items posted by other students.

2. Request

Select items and submit a request form.

3. Pick Up

Arrange a time and pick up your item on campus.

Why It Matters

A small donation can help another student save money and feel supported. This project promotes community, sustainability, and student care.
